About Our School

Our History

NEW ABIREM/AFOSU SENIOR HIGH was established on 28th day of January, 1991.it is located at New Abirem in the Birim North District in the Eastern Region. The school is situated on two traditional lands:New Abirem and Afosu.

Our Mission

To create a congenial environment for effective teaching and learning, to prepare all student for further education, skill training and the world of work to promote national development

Our Vision

We aim to prepare, eqiup and motivate student to become critical thinkers, lifelong learners and responsible citizens in a technologically advanced world

Our Motto

"Unity and Service"
