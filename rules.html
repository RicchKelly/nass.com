Rules & Regulations

General Rules

Attendance & Punctuality

  • School hours are from 6:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  • Extended lessons attendance is compulsory
  • Students must be punctual for all classes
  • Minimum of 80% attendance required per term

Dress Code

Uniform Requirements

  • Complete school uniform must be worn on all school days
  • PE kit is mandatory for sports activities
  • Proper black shoes and white socks required
  • No jewelry or makeup allowed
  • Hair must be neatly groomed

Conduct & Behavior

Expected Behavior

  • Respect for staff and fellow students
  • No fighting or bullying
  • Maintain cleanliness of school premises
  • No use of abusive language
  • Follow classroom etiquette

Prohibited Items

Not Allowed on Campus

  • Mobile phones Note (laptops are allowed for only Elective ICT students)
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Drugs and alcohol
  • Unauthorized reading materials
  • Valuable items not related to studies

Academic Rules

Study Requirements

  • Complete all assignments on time
  • Participate in class activities
  • Maintain minimum grade requirements
  • No cheating or plagiarism
  • Attend all examinations

Boarding Rules

Hostel Regulations

  • Observe lights-out time strictly
  • Keep dormitories clean and tidy
  • No visiting opposite gender dormitories
  • Follow meal time schedules
  • Obtain permission before leaving campus
