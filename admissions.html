Admissions

Welcome to NEW ABIREM/AFOSU SENIOR HIGH

We are delighted that you are considering joining our prestigious institution. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and merit-based, ensuring equal opportunities for all qualified candidates.

Admission Requirements

Basic Requirements

  • BECE Certificate with aggregate 6-30
  • Primary School Records
  • Birth Certificate
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Completed application form

Academic Requirements

  • Science: Minimum of grade 3 in Mathematics and Science
  • General Arts: Minimum of grade 4 in English and Social Studies
  • Visual Arts: Minimum of grade 4 in Creative Arts
  • Home Economics: Minimum of grade 4 in English and Science
  • Agricultural Science: Minimum of grade 4 in Science and Mathematics

Additional Requirements

  • Medical examination form
  • Character reference letter
  • Previous school behavior report
  • Parent/Guardian contact information

Application Process

1

Online Application

Complete the online application form and submit required documents

2

Document Verification

School administration verifies submitted documents

3

Interview

Selected candidates attend interview with parent/guardian

4

Admission Offer

Successful candidates receive admission letter
